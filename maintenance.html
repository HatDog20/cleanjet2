Maintenance Tips

Essential maintenance tips to keep your vehicle running smoothly!

Car Maintenance Tips

  • Regularly check the oil level and change it every 3,000 to 5,000 miles.
  • Inspect your tires monthly for wear and tear, and keep them properly inflated.
  • Replace the air filter at regular intervals to improve engine performance and fuel economy.
  • Maintain your brakes to ensure safety and optimal stopping performance.
  • Check and top off your coolant to prevent engine overheating.

Motorcycle Maintenance Tips

  • Change engine oil regularly (every 2,000 to 3,000 miles for most motorcycles).
  • Ensure your tires are correctly inflated and free from visible damage.
  • Clean and lube the chain every 500 miles or as needed.
  • Check the brake fluid levels and ensure the brakes are functioning correctly.
  • Inspect the battery and clean terminals to prevent corrosion.

General Maintenance Tips

  • Keep your vehicle clean inside and out to prevent wear and tear.
  • Ensure the lights, wipers, and other accessories are working properly.
  • Check your fluid levels (transmission, power steering, and brake fluids).
  • Replace the spark plugs to maintain engine performance.
  • Consult the owner's manual for specific maintenance schedules.
