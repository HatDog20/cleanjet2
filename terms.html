Terms and Services

Effective Date: May 3, 2025

1. Service Scope

CleanJet offers vehicle cleaning services for both motorcycles and cars. We reserve the right to refuse service for any vehicle that may pose a health, safety, or operational risk.

2. Booking and Payment

To confirm a service appointment, customers may be required to make a downpayment. This downpayment ensures your booking slot and will be deducted from the final service fee. In the case of a no-show or late cancellation (less than 2 hours before the scheduled time), the downpayment is non-refundable.

3. Cancellations and Refunds

Cancellations must be made at least 2 hours before the scheduled time. Refunds will only be issued for services not rendered or if cancellation is done within the allowed timeframe.

4. Liability

CleanJet is not liable for pre-existing damage to vehicles. Customers are advised to inspect their vehicles before and after service. We take great care during cleaning, but CleanJet is not responsible for issues resulting from faulty parts or owner modifications.

5. Privacy

We collect basic customer data for booking and contact purposes only. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties.

CleanJet reserves the right to update these terms at any time. Continued use of our services indicates acceptance of the current terms.
